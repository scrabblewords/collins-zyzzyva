Welcome to Collins Zyzzyva !

Collins Zyzzyva is a programme to help you study words and practice your anagramming skills. It allows you to search for words, anagrams, and definitions, as well as give yourself quizzes. Collins Zyzzyva is particularly valuable for learning words for word games. Collins Zyzzyva also has a full screen Word Judge mode, which can be useful for determining the validity of words while playing word games.

Collins Zyzzyva is Free Software, available under the GNU General Public License. The latest version can always be found at Collins.
